About Mangat Family Dentistry

Working Together is Better

Dr. Harcharn S. Mangat founded Mangat Family Dentistry in 1984 and continued to care for the Pine Grove community for thirty-two years. After three decades of growth, Dr. Mangat opened a second location in Womelsdorf in 2015 before retiring in 2016. Now his son, Dr. Harjeet S. Mangat, is carrying on the family tradition of delivering quality oral health services with skill and transparency.

When you walk into our offices, you will be treated like family. You will always know what treatment you will receive, what it will cost, and how it will benefit you before any work is performed.

Your Oral Health Partners

Harjeet Mangat, DMD

Dr. Harjeet Mangat graduated from Temple Kornberg School of Dentistry and is Invisalign certified. He primarily works from our Pine Grove office but also offers implant placement in Womelsdorf. He operates Mangat Family Dentistry with his wife, Dr. Jaskirat Mangat.

Jaskirat K. Mangat, DMD

Dr. Jaskirat K. Mangat graduated from Midwestern University College of Dental Medicine and is an Invisalign Silver Provider. She was raised in California and moved to Pennsylvania after marrying Dr. Harjeet Mangat. She is our main family dentist in Womelsdorf.

Corina L. Torres, DDS

Dr. Corina L.Torres holds a Doctorate in Dental Surgery from West Virginia University. Schuylkill County-born, she now lives in Pottsville with her fiance and two children. She practices at both the Pine Grove and Womelsdorf offices.

OUR LOCATIONS

Two Offices for More Convenience

As a multi-location dental office serving Pine Grove and Womelsdorf, we aim to deliver a consistent patient experience with geographic convenience. Dr. Harjeet Mangat leads our Pine Grove office while Dr. Jaskirat Mangat heads up Womelsdorf. Dr. Torres splits hours between them both. This allows us to offer more clinical hours while maintaining exceptional standards of care across locations.

Our providers collaborate closely to ensure consistency of care, and support each other on complex treatment plans and procedures. Whichever location you find yourself at, you will always be treated like family by the same familiar faces.
